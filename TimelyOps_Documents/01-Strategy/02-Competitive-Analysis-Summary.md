# TimelyOps — Competitive Landscape Summary
*Derived from FSM Software Competitive Analysis (March 2026) — Top 25 platforms*

---

## Where TimelyOps Sits in the Market

TimelyOps is in the SMB Home Services segment alongside Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceM8, and FieldPulse. These are the tools your prospective customers are most likely comparing you against. The enterprise platforms (ServiceTitan, Oracle, SAP) are irrelevant at your current scale and target customer size.

---

## Your Closest Competitors

### Jobber — Most Direct Threat
- $119/month for up to 5 users (Connect plan — the one most SMBs buy)
- Best-in-class UX, strong quoting, digital approvals, automation
- **Your edge:** Jobber charges per user beyond 5, and does not include an AI booking agent on any plan. Flat pricing is a real differentiator.

### Housecall Pro — Mobile-First Alternative
- $49/month for 1 user; adds ~$35/user beyond that
- Very fast onboarding, built-in consumer financing
- **Your edge:** Housecall Pro gets expensive fast for teams. No AI booking agent. Not cleaning-specific.

### ServiceM8 — The Flat-Fee Precedent
- $0–$349/month based on job volume, not users
- iOS-first, great for seasonal teams
- **Your edge:** TimelyOps is cleaning-specific and bilingual. ServiceM8 is general-purpose.

### FieldPulse — Closest Pricing Model
- Flat monthly fee regardless of users
- All core FSM features
- **Your edge:** No AI booking agent. Not cleaning-specific. No bilingual interface.

## The Pricing Opportunity

For a 5-person cleaning team, here's how annual cost compares:

| Platform | Annual Cost (5 staff) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| TimelyOps Essentials | ~$1,188 | Flat fee |
| Jobber Connect | ~$1,428 | Up to 5 users |
| Housecall Pro | ~$2,508+ | Base + 4 extra users |
| FieldPulse | ~$1,428 | Flat fee, similar range |
| ServiceTitan | $15,000–$42,000+ | Overkill for this segment |

TimelyOps is **cost-competitive** at launch and increasingly wins on price as teams grow past 5 people.

## Strategic Recommendations

**Short term:** When doing outreach or listing on G2/Capterra, lead with the AI booking agent and flat pricing. These are your two most unique claims in the SMB cleaning space.

**Medium term:** Automated reminders (reducing no-shows 30–40%) is the single highest-ROI feature you can ship next. The analysis shows this is universally high-value and it's already on your Pro tier roadmap.

**Watch:** ServiceM8 and Workiz both offer job-volume and flat-fee models. If either of them adds an AI booking agent or cleaning-specific features, the competitive gap narrows. First-mover advantage matters here.
