# TimelyOps — Document Consistency Review
**One-Pager v2 vs. Product Briefing v3 vs. Pitch Deck**
*Reviewed: April 2026*

---

## Summary

The One-Pager and Product Briefing are well-aligned in tone and positioning. The **pitch deck has outdated pricing and outdated tier names** — this is the most important issue to fix before sharing it with anyone.

---

## 🔴 Critical Issue — Pitch Deck Pricing is Wrong

The pitch deck still uses old pricing from an earlier version of the product.

| Location | Tier Names | Prices |
|---|---|---|
| **Pitch deck** (current file) | Starter / Professional / Growth | $79 / $129 / $249 |
| **Product Briefing v3** (correct) | Essentials / Pro | $99 / $149 |
| **CLAUDE.md / live platform** (correct) | Essentials / Pro | $99 / $149 |

The pitch deck needs to be updated on **slides 3, 5, 7, and 10** before it's shown to anyone. The tier names "Starter" and "Professional" have been replaced by "Essentials" and "Pro." The Growth tier at $249 also does not appear in the current pricing structure.

---

## 🟡 Inconsistency — "Owner Exocortex" Positioning

The Product Briefing introduces a strong, distinctive positioning statement: *"TimelyOps competes with the owner's own memory (spreadsheets, text messages, mental load), not with generic field service software."* It calls the platform an "owner exocortex."

This idea does not appear in the One-Pager or pitch deck, despite being one of the sharpest ways to explain why TimelyOps is different. Worth weaving into the One-Pager's solution section and pitch deck Slide 2 (The Problem).

---

## 🟡 Minor — One-Pager Has No Pricing

The One-Pager describes the value proposition clearly but never mentions price. This is likely intentional (you don't want stale prices printed in a PDF) — but it means there's no anchor for a prospect reading it cold. Consider adding a line like *"Starting at $99/month — flat fee, no per-user charges"* or a link to the pricing page.

- The connected pipeline concept (quote → approve → schedule → complete → collect → invoice)

1. **Update the pitch deck** — change pricing and tier names on slides 3, 5, 7, and 10. This should be done before any investor or customer presentation.
